# Vision

## Aspiration

Every customer gets exactly the drink they ordered, without waiting in a queue to place that order. Staff focus entirely on making great drinks instead of juggling order-taking, clarification, and payment.

## Longer-term direction

- **Loyalty program** — reward regular customers, track preferences, offer personalised recommendations.
- **Mobile ordering** — order ahead from your phone, skip the kiosk entirely.
- **Sales analytics** — understand peak times, popular combinations, inventory planning.
- **Multi-location support** — if the concept works, expand to additional locations with consistent ordering.
