# Outcomes

## MVP definition

The first version delivers a self-service ordering kiosk where customers can browse the menu, customise a drink, and pay. Orders transmit immediately to a kitchen display where staff can see order details and mark orders as complete. Payment is processed through an external payment provider.

## Success criteria

- A customer can place a fully customised order and pay in under 2 minutes.
- Kitchen staff see the exact order with no ambiguity.
- Zero customisation errors — the order on the kitchen display matches exactly what the customer selected.
- The system handles payment failure gracefully, preserving the customer's order so they can retry or cancel.

## What success does NOT require

- Real payment processing (simulated is sufficient)
- Customer accounts or order history
- Loyalty points or rewards
- Mobile ordering
- Inventory management
- Receipt printing
- Multi-language support
- Accessibility features beyond basic usability
- Multiple kiosk support (single kiosk is fine)
- Analytics or reporting
