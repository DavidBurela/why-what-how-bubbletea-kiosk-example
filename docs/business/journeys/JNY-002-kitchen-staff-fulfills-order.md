# JNY-002 — Kitchen Staff Fulfills an Order

## Persona

[Kitchen Staff — Jamie](../personas/kitchen-staff.md)

## Goal

Prepare the exact drink the customer ordered, with clear and unambiguous order information.

## Trigger

A new order appears on the kitchen display.

## Steps

1. Kitchen staff sees a new order appear on the kitchen display.
2. Staff reviews the full order details: drink name, milk type, toppings, sugar level, ice level, temperature.
3. Staff prepares the drink following the displayed specifications.
4. Staff marks the order as complete on the kitchen display.
5. Staff verbally calls out the order number for the customer to pick up.

## Success criteria

Staff can read the order at a glance without ambiguity. No verbal relay, no handwritten notes, no guessing.
