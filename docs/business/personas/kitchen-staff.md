# Kitchen Staff — Jamie

## Who they are

Works the preparation station at Compile & Sip. Makes the drinks. Has been there for 8 months and knows the menu inside out. Good at the craft but struggles when orders come in garbled or incomplete.

## What they care about

- **Clear, unambiguous order information** — they need to know exactly what to make.
- **Steady workflow** — a predictable stream of orders is better than a burst of 10 orders followed by nothing.
- **Not being blamed for errors that originated at the counter.**

## What they want from this system

- See each order's full details on a screen — drink, size, milk, toppings, sugar, ice, temperature.
- Work through orders in sequence.
- Mark orders as done so the customer is notified.

## Pain points

- Frequently gets incomplete verbal orders relayed from the counter ("Classic milk tea... I think they said oat milk?").
- Peak hour chaos means scribbled notes, shouted modifications, and inevitable mistakes.
- Feels responsible for errors even when the order was taken incorrectly.
