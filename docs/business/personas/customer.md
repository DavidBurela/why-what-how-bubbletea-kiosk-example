# Customer — Alex

## Who they are

A regular at Compile & Sip. Comes in 2–3 times a week, usually during lunch or after work. Knows what they like but also enjoys trying new combinations. Comfortable with self-service technology — uses self-checkout at supermarkets, orders food on apps.

## What they care about

- **Accuracy** — they have specific preferences (oat milk, 50% sugar, less ice) and it's frustrating when the order comes out wrong.
- **Speed** — often on a lunch break or heading somewhere. Waiting 10 minutes in a queue just to place an order is a dealbreaker.
- **Browsing at their own pace** — sometimes they want to try something new but feel rushed when there's a queue behind them.

## What they want from this system

- Walk up, pick their drink, customise it exactly, pay, and go wait for it to be made.
- See all the options clearly without having to ask a staff member.
- Know that what they ordered is what they'll get.

## Pain points

- Has walked away from the shop multiple times when the queue was too long.
- Has received the wrong milk type twice in the last month.
- Feels awkward asking staff to remake a drink even when it's clearly wrong.
